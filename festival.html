ABOUT THE

FESTIVAL


INDIE UN-FILM FESTIVAL (IUF) is an annual intercollegiate digital film festival organized by the DLSU Green Media Group. This event is a platform for student directors to showcase their works to the public for the audiences to deepen their appreciation for the arts. Entering its 17th year, it is considered the longest-running collegiate film festival in the Philippines
