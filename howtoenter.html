Judging Process

  1. All entries will be filtered by the organizers along with selected judges to determine the entries which will qualify for the awards and be shown on the screening. The IUF Selection Committee reserves the right to award the finalist status to any entry.
  2. Decisions of the judges are final with respect to all matters relating to the IUF 2020 film competition. The judges reserve the right to not give any awards in a category should no entry merit it.
  3. In the event that only one entry will qualify in a category, the entry will automatically be declared as the winner; however, the participant will only receive half of the cash prize.
  4. In the case that none of the films of the finalists will qualify after the judges' deliberations, there will be no declared winner. The cash prize of the category shall be equally distributed in the other categories.
  5. Finalists will be notified through text message and email, as well as our Facebook page, and will be given 2 complimentary festival passes. The complimentary passes will be given on the day of the event if and only if the finalists have confirmed attendance.
